Forecasting "Neg Storms": Time-Aware Modeling of Toxic Situations in Social Media

Irien Akter, Vivek K. Singh, Pradeep K. Atrey

IEEE International Symposium on Multimedia (ISM), Naples, Italy, pp. 300–307, 2025

View on IEEE Xplore DOI: 10.1109/ISM66958.2025.00063

Introduced Comment Storm Severity (CSS), a time-aware metric for predicting toxicity escalation from early conversation signals — using only the first 10 comments in a thread. Covered by multiple outlets, including News10-ABC and Newswise.
